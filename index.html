Compress Photo Under 100KB for SSC, Railway & Banking Exams

Government exam forms like SSC, Railway and Banking require candidates to upload passport size photos within strict size limits such as 20KB, 50KB or 100KB. Many applications get rejected due to wrong image size or high file size. EasyFile Tools helps you compress photos online to the exact KB range without losing important clarity. This tool works directly in your browser, so your images are not uploaded to any server.

You can also resize images for normal documents such as Aadhaar, PAN, passport, resumes and online forms. Choose a preset size for exams or enter a custom target size between 20KB and 500KB. The compressor will automatically adjust quality to get the closest possible size. This saves time and avoids repeated trial-and-error while filling forms.
